Why all the hate for Java?

 Published on December 9, 2019 by Maxim Atanasov

Java has been one of the top languages to dislike basically since it's inception. And while the language definetly has it's shortcomings, I don't think it deserves all of the hate it gets. Yes, the language is (very) verbose, yes, it's not as fast as compiled languages and YES, there are too many ServiceConfigurationBeanFactories, but I'll try to make a case for the language and it's surrounding ecosystem anyway.
